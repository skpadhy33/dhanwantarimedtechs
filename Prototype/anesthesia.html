Anesthesia Workstation - CFlow-9

Salient Features

  • Anesthesia machine with Three Gas System (Air,O2 & N2O)
  • Integrated advanced ventilator with full range of modes VCV, PCV, SIMV, PSV with backup Ventilation, Manual and Standby
  • 9 Inch Color TFT Display with Touch & Track operation
  • Advanced proportional valve technology for precise flow control
  • Auto compensated Tidal Volume provides accurate delivery from 20ml-2000ml
  • Smart Driving Gas Shift Technology: In case main Air driving gas fails, atomically shift to Secondary driving gas Oxygen & Visa Versa
  • Simultaneously display waveforms (PT,FT,VT) & Loops ( PV,FV)
  • Electronic PEEP & Fio2 Monitoring
  • Emergency oxygen flish. Flow between 35 to 70 LMP
  • Twin Select a tec type vaporizers manifold
  • Inbuilt Yokes for O2 & N2O
  • Switchable Auxiliary Common gas outlet ACGO)
  • Inbuilt Dual Drawers with big storage space
  • Audible Gas failure Alarm
  • Lithium Ion Battery backup 2 hours
  • Integrated circle observer (1.5 L) with APL Valve and manual/Vent switch for easy operation
