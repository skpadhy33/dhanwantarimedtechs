Dhanwantari Medical Technologies has been established at Bhubaneswar, Odisha during the Year 2019 with a vision of delivering medical devices of innovative, advanced and reliable technology to the Healthcare Industry to facilitate better health-care services by our precious customers.

Since the inception of the company, we have marked our significant presence in the healthcare industry by virtue of our dedicated service and strong commitment to meet customers need.

We are technical tie-up with various leading Indian and Foreign Manufacturers to serve with the Industry.

During the Noval Coronavirus pandemic, we have supplied Hi-End ICU ventilators manufactured by M/s. Cardiolabs Healthcare to various COVID-19 hospitals in all over India.

Our vision is to develop new innovative technology for the critical care division to facilitate life support to all patient group.
